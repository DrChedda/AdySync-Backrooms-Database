Random Generation & Anomalies

Noclip Points

Noclip Points are phenomena found randomly across Level-0. They are easily distinguishable due to their discoloration, glitchy aura, unique sound, and odd architectural composition. Approaching the point too closely will lead you to noclip at random.

Pink Windows

Pink Windows are rooms lit with pink light capable of killing mold and bacterial entities like tripods and mold zombies. They can be used as evasive resting spots or combat leverage.

Trap Rooms

Red-lit, dead-end rooms. If a traveler surpasses the wall boundary, the wall rapidly closes. If trapped, the floor opens into a black pit filled with no-clip particles, leading to certain death. Avoid these rooms.

Crawlspaces

Pocket rooms in Level-0’s complex structure that act as ideal spots for evading entities or gunfire.

Territory & Faction Strongholds

A massive expanse previously occupied by the Away Insurgency. Contains an armory, medbay, sleeping quarters, and a hidden underground bunker. Following a raid on June 7th, 2025, it is now M.E.G. property and serves as a UFA Headquarters.
